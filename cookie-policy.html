Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to enhance the user experience and to enable certain functionality. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to remember your preferences and to improve the website's performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They are used to manage user sessions and to store user preferences.
  • Analytic Cookies: These cookies allow us to analyze website traffic and to measure the performance of our website. They help us to understand how visitors interact with our website and to identify areas for improvement.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements to our visitors. They track your browsing habits and allow us to show you ads that are tailored to your interests.

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that certain parts of the website may not function properly if you do so.
